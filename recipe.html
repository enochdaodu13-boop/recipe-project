Pounded Yam with Egusi Soup

Pounded Yam with Egusi Soup is a very delicious, and good looking food which can be paired with soup like egusi, and it can be served in any occassion.

Recipe Information

Prep time: 10 minutes

Cook time: 20 minutes

Servings: 3 people

Difficulty: Intermediate

Ingredients

  • 1 tuber of yam
  • 2 bundles of spinach (ugu)
  • 2 cups of ground melon seeds (egusi)
